Lovely

She sat on a motorbike, Tight white tanktop

Unassuming unconcealing unrevealing, Loose shorts touch her knees

Shaggy hair slicked back effortlessly

The overgrown buzz cut on the sides

She has time and money , To get it redone

But she doesn't

Hey ladies, she doesn't much care if you respond or not

Gives you her hand, My name is Lovely

And She is
